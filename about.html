About the Trainer

Dedicated to nurturing confident communicators and successful learners.

Manisha Vyas

Manisha Vyas is an enthusiastic educator and trainer who is committed to helping students develop strong English language skills through systematic learning, practical application and continuous guidance. Her learner-centred teaching methodology focuses on building confidence, improving communication skills and strengthening conceptual understanding of English grammar, literature and writing.

She holds a Master of Arts (M.A.) in English Literature, a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), and a Certificate in Child Psychology and Counselling. She has also qualified CTET, REET, and CSB, demonstrating her academic competence and professional preparedness in the field of education.

Alongside her expertise in English language teaching, she has completed a Second M.A. in Yoga and Science of Living and is also a Reiki Master Healer. These qualifications enrich her teaching approach by promoting concentration, emotional well-being, positive thinking and holistic development among learners.

Her mission is not merely to prepare students for examinations, but to help them become confident speakers, effective writers, critical thinkers and lifelong learners capable of using English successfully in academic, professional and social environments.

Teaching Philosophy

Every learner possesses unique strengths and learning styles. Therefore, teaching should inspire curiosity, confidence and independent thinking rather than memorisation alone. At English Tutorials, learning is made interactive, enjoyable and meaningful through practical activities, regular practice, constructive feedback and individual attention. Students are encouraged to ask questions, communicate freely and develop the confidence to use English naturally in real-life situations.
