About Our Centre

Yoga, Meditation & Reiki Healing Centre, Ajmer, is dedicated to promoting holistic health through the timeless practices of Yoga, Meditation, Reiki Healing and Vedic Yagya. Our mission is to help every individual attain physical fitness, emotional stability, mental clarity and spiritual harmony.

The centre provides a peaceful environment where ancient Indian wisdom is blended with modern wellness practices to inspire a healthier and happier lifestyle.

Dr. Vishwas Pareek

Dr. Vishwas Pareek is a renowned Yoga Therapist and Yagyacharya based in Ajmer. With decades of experience in Yoga, therapeutic practices and Vedic traditions, he has guided countless individuals toward better health and spiritual well-being.

His expertise includes Yoga Therapy, Pranayama, Meditation, Vedic Yagya and holistic healing techniques that promote physical vitality, mental peace and spiritual growth.

Manisha Vyas

Manisha Vyas is an Educator, Reiki Master Healer, Yoga Instructor and Counsellor. She believes in nurturing the mind, body and soul through compassionate guidance and holistic wellness practices.

Her areas of expertise include Reiki Healing, Yoga, Meditation, Emotional Wellness, Stress Management and Personal Counselling. She is committed to helping individuals achieve inner peace, confidence and a balanced lifestyle.

Our Vision

To create a healthier society by spreading awareness about Yoga, Meditation, Reiki Healing and holistic living, enabling people to experience harmony, happiness and lifelong wellness.
